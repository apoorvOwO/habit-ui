Habit Tracker Suite

A beautiful, local-first productivity app to build lasting habits, plan projects, and stay on track — no account required, everything saves to your browser.

Built by Apurv Sahay & Anshuman Modgil

✨ All Features

📅

Habit Grid

A visual grid of squares for every day — tap a square to mark it done. Inspired by GitHub contribution graphs.

🔥

Streak Counter

Automatically calculates your current consecutive-day streak to keep motivation high.

⭕

Progress Ring

An animated circular progress ring shows your overall completion percentage at a glance.

⏳

Deadline Tracking

Add an optional target date when creating a habit. A countdown badge shows days remaining — or if you're past deadline.

📊

Analytics Chart

Toggle a monthly bar chart to see which months you were most consistent throughout the year.

🎯

Milestones

Add sub-goals to each habit and check them off as you hit them.

📝

Log & Notes

Keep a timestamped daily journal for each habit — great for reflection and noting what worked.

🌙

Light / Dark Mode

Switch between a rich dark glassmorphism theme and a soft light theme. Preference is remembered.

📤

CSV Export

Download all your habit stats as a spreadsheet for records or analysis in Excel / Google Sheets.

📋

Kanban Board

Manage multi-step projects with a drag-and-drop board across To Do, In Progress, and Done columns.

🗂️

Multiple Projects

Create as many Kanban projects as you need and switch between them instantly with tabs.

💾

Auto-Save

Everything is saved automatically to your browser's local storage — no sign-up, no cloud, fully private.
